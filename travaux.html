Passatemps

Passatemps was my first attempt at doing a website. The goal was to do a website from scratch, doing all the research and the thinking for the visuals, doing the mock up and then of course coding the website itself.
My group and i decided to keep the project alive because we liked the idea of it : a second-hand board game sales site. It is now an on going project that we will continue during our second year of computer science. During our first year, we worked on the project management, thinking about every aspect and doing a functional specifications to prepare the actual programming of the project. We are still in the very begining of the process, thinking about the database structure, how we are going to managed our sprints using the scrum method and learning new programming languages.
